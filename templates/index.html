About this app

Architecture

  • Backend: Python 3 + Flask. Real market data via yfinance.
  • Indicators: RSI(14), MACD(12,26,9), Bollinger Bands(20,2), MA20/50, volume ratio — all computed in app.py → compute_indicators().
  • Simulation: Replays real historical price paths day by day. Applies stop-loss and take-profit on each tick. Generates buy signals using your chosen strategy.
  • Strategies: AI Hybrid, Momentum, Mean Reversion, Breakout, Trend Following, Sentiment. Each is a standalone function in app.py — easy to extend.
  • Export: /api/export returns a formatted .xlsx with 4 sheets: Summary, Trade Log, Equity Curve, Open Positions.

Extending

  • Add new strategies: write a signal_yourname(ind) function and register it in STRATEGY_SIGNALS.
  • Add real sentiment: replace the random stub in signal_sentiment() with a real NLP call (e.g. FinBERT, OpenAI, or news API).
  • Add more sectors: extend the SECTORS dict at the top of app.py.
  • Persist results: swap the in-memory flow for a SQLite database (see TODO comments).
  • Schedule auto-runs: use APScheduler or a cron job calling /api/simulate.
